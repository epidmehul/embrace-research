We are looking for clinicians, healthcare administrators, and community members to inform and disseminate our research.

Who We're Looking For

This research is stronger with you

EMBRACE is a collaborative at its core. Our work is shaped by the perspectives of people who live and work within the systems we study — and it only reaches its potential when those voices are part of the process.

Whether you're a clinician treating patients, an administrator managing a health system, or a community member who has experienced emergency care firsthand, we want to hear from you.

🩺

Clinicians & EMS Providers

Physicians, nurses, paramedics, and EMTs who work in emergency and prehospital settings.

🏛️

Healthcare Administrators

Hospital leaders, EMS directors, and health system administrators involved in emergency care.

🤝

Community Members

Patients, caregivers, and community advocates who can share lived experiences with emergency care.

🔬

Researchers & Collaborators

Academics and research teams interested in partnership or joint projects on emergency systems.
